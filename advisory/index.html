Advisory

Practical advice for technical and transformation decisions that carry real weight.

Arkham Advisory works where architecture, delivery, data, AI, and organisational reality have to be considered together. The aim is not more process. It is clearer judgment at the moment decisions become expensive.

01

Architecture and platform strategy

For organisations deciding how their platforms should evolve when scale, ownership, integration, and delivery pressure are all moving at once.

Example situations

  • A platform direction has stalled between competing technical, product, and operating-model views.
  • A leadership team needs an independent view before committing to a major architecture decision.
  • A data or engineering platform has grown useful, but its boundaries and ownership are becoming unclear.

Likely outputs

  • Architecture decision support
  • Platform direction assessment
  • Independent strategic review
  • Leadership alignment workshop

02

AI strategy and agentic systems

For leaders moving past demos into situations where AI is actually touching delivery, decisions, documentation, or how systems behave.

Example situations

  • AI pilots are multiplying faster than policy, architecture, and delivery controls can absorb.
  • Agentic workflows are promising, but the organisation is unsure which boundaries, records, and approvals matter.
  • Engineering teams need clarity on how AI changes requirements, documentation, review, and ownership.

Likely outputs

  • AI operating model review
  • Agentic workflow risk assessment
  • Decision and governance principles
  • Practical adoption roadmap

03

Technical due diligence

For investors, boards, executives, or sponsors who need a clear view of what a technology organisation can actually sustain.

Example situations

  • An acquisition, investment, or partnership requires an independent technical and delivery view.
  • A transformation programme needs scrutiny before another major funding or scope decision.
  • A product or platform appears healthy on dashboards, but leadership wants to understand hidden structural risk.

Likely outputs

  • Technical value assurance
  • Architecture and delivery risk report
  • Executive findings briefing
  • Remediation and confidence plan

04

Transformation counsel

For organisations where transformation depends on hard technical choices, cross-functional alignment, and a sharper view of delivery truth.

Example situations

  • A major programme is moving, but confidence is uneven and the real constraints are hard to name.
  • Leadership needs a calm external view on delivery, architecture, data, and organisational dependency risk.
  • A strategy is sound in principle but needs translation into decisions that teams can execute.

Likely outputs

  • Delivery confidence review
  • Transformation steering support
  • Strategic decision framing
  • Sponsor and leadership counsel

Engagement formats

A shape for the decision in front of you.

The format depends on the level of uncertainty, urgency, and leadership attention required. Each engagement is designed to produce useful clarity rather than generic theatre.

Advisory retainer

Ongoing senior counsel for leaders navigating a sequence of architectural, AI, data, or transformation decisions.

Focused assessment

A short, sharp review of a platform, programme, decision, or risk area where clarity is needed quickly.

Strategic review

A deeper independent view on technology direction, delivery confidence, organisational readiness, and value risk.

Workshop or deep dive

A structured session to align leaders, expose assumptions, and turn ambiguity into decisions and next actions.
